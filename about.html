About Us

Jaisalmer, also known as the Golden City, is a beautiful desert city in Rajasthan that attracts travellers from all over the world. We understand that travellers want to experience Jaisalmer uniquely and that's why your ideal travel partner offers non-touristic safari options that will allow you to witness the exotic and enigmatic sites with the beauty of Jaisalmer like never before.

At Sandscape Voyages Jaisalmar, we have a team of professionals who are local to the area and have extensive knowledge about the city's history, culture, traditions and desert area. Our team of seasoned tour guides is passionate about what they do and will take you on an offbeat journey that won't be forgotten. Our non-touristic safari packages include picturesque sightseeing with camel safari in a far-stretched desert area, doing the most common things uncommonly with offbeat tracks and promoting healthy tourism in Jaisalmer.

We are a dedicated tour operator based in Rajasthan, especially Jaisalmer, with more than 15 years of experience in providing personalised and unforgettable travel experiences to our clients from all over the world. Our commitment to customer satisfaction is unparalleled. We offer much more than just Desert Safari packages, Desert Camp, Camel Safari, Jeep Safari, and Jaisalmer Tours - we promise to deliver an experience that will stay with you for a lifetime.

At Karan Desert Safari, we pride ourselves on being the pioneers of luxury in Jaisalmer. Our renowned service and exceptional facilities are sure to leave you with a fresh perspective on the Golden City. We offer authentic and world-class hospitality, ensuring that customers have a comfortable and enjoyable trip. We provide the best hotels and tents to make your stay in Jaisalmer hassle-free.

We understand that every traveller is unique and has different needs and preferences. That's why we work closely with our clients to create a customised itinerary that suits their interests, budgets and schedules. Our TripAdvisor reputation and Google reviews say it all. We aim to help tourists explore the beauty of Jaisalmer and create an unforgettable travel experience. Do not miss out on the opportunity to explore this breathtaking destination with your loved ones. Come visit and experience the magic of Jaisalmer firsthand.

Message from Yuvraj Singh Bhati

Welcome to Sandscape Voyages Jaisalmer! I’m Yuvraj Singh, a proud resident of the Golden City, Jaisalmer. For the past 15 years, I have been deeply connected with the world of tourism and hospitality, sharing the timeless charm of Rajasthan with travelers from across the globe. My journey began with a simple dream — to show the real beauty of Jaisalmer beyond the usual tourist routes. Over the years, I’ve worked as a tour operator, guide, and travel planner, offering a complete range of personalized services including city tours, hotel bookings, desert safaris, taxi and transfer services, camel and jeep rides, and private sightseeing trips.

At Sandscape Voyages, we focus on authentic and non-touristic desert experiences—far from the crowds, where guests can truly feel the silence and magic of the Thar Desert. Our special highlights include traditional Rajasthani dinners on the sand dunes under the stars, cultural performances, and cozy overnight stays in small camps or luxury tents that blend comfort with nature.

My team and me, are passionate about creating warm, memorable experiences—whether you wish to explore the historic Jaisalmer Fort and Jain Temples, walk through the golden streets of the old city, or spend a peaceful night in the desert. With Sandscape Voyages, you’re not just a traveler—you’re our guest and a part of our Jaisalmer family.

Make your tour memorable with us.
